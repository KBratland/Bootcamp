Our School

Children at Young Wonders Preschool are encouraged to discover and create through play. We believe that play is the foundation for learning. Valuable life skills including cooperation, problem solving, and self-confidence are fostered through a range of open-ended activities, structured group experiences and respectful guidance.

Eco-friendly Childcare

As an Eco-Health Child Care certified preschool, we emphasize a non-toxic environment, natural and nature-based materials and time each day spent outside exploring our world.

The Garden

Nature is a fundamental need of children. We have designed the garden to ensure that children can experience and absorb the natural world around them. We are in the garden every day, rain or shine.

Becoming a part of the Young Wonders family. We'd like to meet you!

With every student the community changes. It's a big deal to us and we make sure everyone is a good fit. To do this we meet with you the parents, then the child or children in the class room with you. We also do a home visit with our lead teacher and then several integration days. We strive for your child's success from before her first day of school to past his last.

A day at Young Wonders. It's changing everyday!

Young Wonders’ students have the opportunity on any given day to bead a necklace, paint at the easel, make a puzzle, play with friends, build with blocks, work in the garden, eat a healthy snack, write in their journals, be read to, sing songs, play dress-up, rest, run and grow. Providing children with choices allows them to direct their own learning experiences.

About our director. Christine Whitmore

Christine Whitmore has worked with young children since 1993. She has a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology with a minor in Environmental Studies. She also obtained an Associates Degree in Early Childhood Education in 1998. Her gentle and holistic approach to educating young children emphasizes a multidisciplinary background and a broad understanding of early childhood development. She has taken additional coursework in Parenting with Positive Discipline, Healthy Children - Healthy Planet and Music Together. The Young Wonders environment reflects her love of gardening, creative pursuits and a desire to share her passion for sustainable living.
